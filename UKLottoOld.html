UK Lotto Game Rules

To play Lotto, simply select 6 numbers from 1 to 59 or choose a Quick Play. To win the jackpot, all 6 main numbers must be matched. If there’s more than one jackpot winner, the prize will be shared equally between all jackpot winners. The minimum jackpots are estimated at £2 million on Wednesdays and £4 million on Saturdays. Draws take place on Wednesday and Saturday evenings UK time.

UK Lotto Jackpot Rollovers

Every UK Lotto draw cycle has a starting jackpot of $3 million. If nobody wins the jackpot there will be a rollover and the jackpot will increase in size. The rollover will continue until the jackpot is won, or the jackpot cap of $40 million is reached.

Once the jackpot cap is reached and nobody wins the jackpot, the second tier prizes are supplemented.

UK Lotto Winnings Tax

All lottery winnings claimed through Eureka Lottos are subjected to Australian tax laws.
