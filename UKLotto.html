Summary of Lottery Operator Rules

Selection of Numbers

To play UK Lotto, a player selects six numbers from a pool of 1 to 59. Players may select their own numbers or use the ‘quick pick’ function to have the numbers randomly selected.

Prizes and Odds

There are six different ways for players to win a prize.

Prize Tier Match Estimated Prizes Odds
1 6 Numbers Jackpot 1 in 45,057,474
2 5 Numbers + Bonus Number £1,000,000 1 in 7,509,579
3 5 Numbers £1,750 1 in 144,415
4 4 Numbers £140 1 in 2,180
5 3 Numbers £30 1 in 96.2
6 2 Numbers Free Lotto Lucky Dip 1 in 10.3
The overall odds of winning a prize are 1 in 9.3.

The underlying lottery is conducted on a pari-mutuel lottery basis, with the exception of the match 2 numbers prize (Prize Tier 6), how much you win depends on the number of winning tickets in that same tier as well as the number of tickets sold in the particular underlying lottery draw.

The ‘Lotto Lucky Dip’ prize is for players who win the match 2 main numbers prize category (Prize Tier 6) will receive a free ‘Lucky Dip’ game in the next draw, a ‘Lucky Dip’ uses a ‘quick pick’ function to randomly select the numbers.

Only one prize per game can be won in each draw, if a game qualifies as a winner across more than one prize category than the highest prize will be paid to the winning player.

Prize capping takes place in very rare circumstances where popular number combinations are drawn, resulting in more winners than the operator of the underlying operator is able to pay for. In such cases the prizes won for matching 3, 4, 5 or 6 main numbers or 5 numbers and the bonus ball (Prize Tiers 1 to 5) may be less than stated.

Draw Times

UK Lotto draws are conducted twice each week, at 8:00p.m. on Wednesdays and Saturdays at 7:45p.m. (Greenwich Mean Time). The draw time converts to Thursdays and Sundays, Australian Eastern Standard Time (AEST)

Jackpot Rollover

There is a jackpot cap on UK Lotto, jackpot rollovers are limited to five draws, on the fifth rollover that draw is declared a ‘Must Be Won Draw’ and the jackpot if not won is ‘rolled down’ and shared between all prize winners.

In a ‘Must Be Won Draw’ where there is no jackpot winner, players who have a winning ticket in the Match 2 Prize Category (Prize Tier 6) will receive £5 in addition to the usual ‘Lotto Lucky Dip’ prize, the increase to the other prize categories are distributed in the following percentages:

Match Allocation of Jackpot
by Prize Category
5 Numbers + Bonus Number 3%
5 Numbers 5%
4 Numbers 7%
3 Numbers 85%

In the event that there is not a winner in a particular prize category, the operator and regulator of the underlying lottery will adjust and determine the percentage to be distributed to the other prize categories.

Jackpot Payout

Jackpot winners receive their prize as a lump-sum payment.

Tax

Lottery winnings are not currently taxed in the United Kingdom, where the matching tickets are purchased in the underlying lottery.

Promotional Prizes

We are unable to offer any local promotional prizes that are offered by the underlying lottery operators from time to time. The six different ways for players to win a prize as listed above are the valid prizes we offer.
