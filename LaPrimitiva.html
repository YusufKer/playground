Summary of Lottery Operator Rules

Selection of Numbers

To play La Primitiva, a player selects six numbers from a pool of 1 to 49 and one Reintegro Number from a pool of 0 to 9. Players may select their own numbers or use the ‘quick pick’ function to have the numbers randomly selected.

Prizes and Odds

There are seven different ways for players to win a prize.

The Complimentary Number is an additional seventh ball drawn that is used to determine winners of third prize tier.

The underlying lottery is conducted on a pari-mutuel lottery basis, how much you win depends on the number of winning tickets in that same tier as well as the number of tickets sold in the particular underlying lottery draw, with the exception of the match 3 Number (Prize Tier 6) and Reintegro (Prize tier 7) that are fixed amount prizes.

The ‘Joker’ is a supplementary raffle game where a player can purchase a ‘Joker’ number which is a seven-digit number randomly assigned to the La Primitiva ticket. The ‘Joker’ number is drawn after the La Primitiva draw, there are seven different prize tiers in the ‘Joker’ draw.

Only one prize per game can be won in each draw, if a game qualifies as a winner across more than one prize category than the highest prize will be paid to the winning player.

Draw Times

La Primitiva draws are conducted twice a week on Thursdays and Saturdays at approximately 9:40pm (Central European Time - CET). The draw time converts to Fridays and Saturdays, Australian Eastern Standard Time (AEST).

Jackpot Rollover

There is no jackpot cap on La Primitiva, the jackpot continues to grow until it is won.

Jackpot Payout

Jackpot winners receive their prize as a lump-sum payment.

Tax

The Spanish government apply a tax of 20% on lottery winnings greater than €40,000. This tax is deducted from the prize amount prior to the payout from the underlying lottery operator.

Promotional Prizes

We are unable to offer any local promotional prizes that are offered by the underlying lottery operators from time to time. The prizes as listed above are the valid prizes we offer.
