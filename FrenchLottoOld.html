French Loto Game Rules

The French Loto is a lottery with draws taking place every Monday, Wednesday and Saturday. Players need to choose five main numbers from a 1-49 number pool, and one additional bonus number from a 1-10 number pool.

The French Loto has a starting jackpot of $3 million and will rollover for every draw that it is not won.

French Loto Jackpot Rollovers

The French Loto has a starting jackpot of $3 million and a rollover occurs if there is no jackpot winner. When a rollover happens the jackpot will get bigger until it is won, or until the jackpot cap of $36 million is reached.
