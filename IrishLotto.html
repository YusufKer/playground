Summary of Lotter Operator Rules

Selection of Numbers

To play Irish Lotto, a player selects six numbers from a pool of 1 to 47. Players may select their own numbers or use the ‘quick pick’ function to have the numbers randomly selected.

Prizes and Odds

There are seven different ways for players to win a prize.

The overall odds of winning a prize are 1 in 29.

The jackpot for the underlying lottery is conducted on a pari-mutuel lottery basis, how much you win depends on the number of winning tickets in that same tier as well as the number of tickets sold in the particular underlying lottery draw.

Only one prize per game can be won in each draw, if a game qualifies as a winner across more than one prize category than the highest prize will be paid to the winning player.

In the event that there is not a winner in a particular non-jackpot prize category, the prize fund allocated for that prize category will be rolled over or rolled down in the following way:

Draw Times

Irish Lotto draws are conducted twice each week on Wednesdays and Saturdays at 7:55p.m. (Irish Standard Time). The draw time converts to Thursdays and Sundays, Australian Eastern Standard Time (AEST).

Jackpot Rollover

There is a jackpot cap on Irish Lotto, the minimum jackpot is €2 million and continues to grow until it reaches the jackpot cap, known as the “Current Jackpot Prize Cap’, currently set at €18.96 million. If the jackpot reaches this amount and the jackpot is not won, any amount that would normally go towards increasing the jackpot is rolled down to the next prize category with at least one winner for that draw.

In the event that the jackpot prize is won at an amount higher than the ‘Current Jackpot Prize Cap’ than the winning amount will become the ‘Current Jackpot Prize Cap’ for each subsequent draw.

Jackpot Payout

Jackpot winners receive their prize as a lump-sum payment.

Tax

Lottery winnings are not currently taxed in Ireland, where the matching tickets are purchased in the underlying lottery.

Promotional Prizes

We are unable to offer any local promotional prizes that are offered by the underlying lottery operators from time to time. The seven different ways for players to win a prize as listed above are the valid prizes we offer.
