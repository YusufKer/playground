Irish Lotto Game Rules

The Irish Lotto is available to play twice a week, with draws taking place every Wednesday and Saturday at approximately 8pm Dublin time. Players must pick six lucky numbers, including the bonus number, from a number pool of 1-47. For each drawing 6 numbers are randomly selected and a single additional bonus number.

Irish Lotto Jackpot Rollovers

If no player has matched all the lucky numbers needed to claim the jackpot then a jackpot rollover occurs and the jackpot prize for the following draw becomes bigger.

The jackpot rollover continues for each draw that it is not won until the jackpot cap is reached. If there is still no winner once the jackpot cap is reached, the rollover will go toward increasing the prize value of the next tier of which there is at least one winner.
