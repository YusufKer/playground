La Primitiva Lotto Game Rules

To play La Primitiva lottery online simply select six numbers from a range of 1-49. A Reintegro number between 0 and 9 will be printed on the ticket. During the draw, six numbers are selected at random, followed by a bonus ball, called a Complementario, out of the remaining 43 balls. Finally, the Reintegro is drawn from a separate machine.
