Summary of Lottery Operator Rules

Selection of Numbers

To play EuroJackpot, a player selects five numbers from a pool of 1 to 50 and then two Euro numbers from a pool of 1 to 10. Players may select their own numbers or use the ‘quick pick’ function to have the numbers randomly selected

The overall odds of winning a prize are 1 in 26.

The remaining 12% of the prize fund is allocated to a reserve Booster Fund to ensure the minimum jackpot can always be paid. If the Booster Fund exceeds €20 million, any extra funds are diverted into the jackpot for the next draw.

The underlying lottery is conducted on a pari-mutuel lottery basis, how much you win depends on the number of winning tickets in that same tier as well as the number of tickets sold in the particular underlying lottery draw.

Only one prize per game can be won in each draw, if a game qualifies as a winner across more than one prize category than the highest prize will be paid to the winning player.

Draw Times

We are unable to offer any local promotional prizes that are offered by the underlying lottery operators from time to time. The 12 different ways for players to win a prize as listed above are the valid prizes we offer.

EuroJackpot draws are conducted each week, on Fridays at 9:00p.m. (Eastern European Time). The draw time converts to Saturdays, Australian Eastern Standard Time (AEST).

Jackpot Rollover

There is a jackpot cap on EuroJackpot, the minimum jackpot is €10 million and continues to grow until it reaches the jackpot cap, currently set at €90 million. If the jackpot reaches this amount and the jackpot is not won, any amount that would normally go towards increasing the jackpot is rolled down to the second division prize pool.

Jackpot Payout

Jackpot winners receive their prize as a lump-sum payment.

Tax

Lottery winnings are not currently taxed in Germany, where the matching tickets are purchased in the underlying lottery.

Promotional Prizes

We are unable to offer any local promotional prizes that are offered by the underlying lottery operators from time to time. The 12 different ways for players to win a prize as listed above are the valid prizes we offer.
