USA Powerball Game Rules

General Overview:

USA Powerball is one of the most popular lottery games in the world, offering nine ways to win a prize.

All prizes are set cash amounts with the exception of the grand prize. The minimum USA Powerball jackpot is US$40 million and with no jackpot cap it continues to increase each draw until it is won.

Summary of Lottery Operator Rules

Selection of Numbers

To play Powerball, a player selects five numbers from a pool of 1 to 69 and then selects a single Powerball number from a pool of 1 to 26. Players may select their own numbers or use the ‘quick pick’ function to have the numbers randomly selected.

The overall odds of winning a prize are 1 in 24.87

The jackpot of the underlying lottery is on a pari-mutuel lottery basis, how much you win depends on the number of winning tickets in that same tier as well as the number of tickets sold in the particular underlying lottery draw.

Only one prize per game can be won in each draw, if a game qualifies as a winner across more than one prize category than the highest prize will be paid to the winning player.

Draw Times

Powerball draws are conducted twice each week, on Wednesdays and Saturdays with each draw taking place at 10:59 p.m. (United States Eastern Time). The draw time converts to Thursdays and Sundays, Australian Eastern Standard Time (AEST).

Jackpot Rollovers

There is no jackpot cap on USA Powerball, the jackpot continues to grow until it is won.

Jackpot Payout

The jackpot prize in the underlying lottery is on a pari-mutuel basis, how much you win depends on the number of winning tickets in that same tier as well as the number of tickets sold in a particular draw.

Jackpot winners can elect to receive their prize as an annuity or a lump-sum payment.

Annuity

Jackpot winners who select the annuity option will receive 30 payments over 29 years, one initial payment with each following annual payment increasing at a rate of 5% per year.

The total of the 30 payments is equal to the value of the annuity.

Lump-Sum Payment

Jackpot winners who select the lump-sum payout will receive a single payment, this payment will always be an amount less than the advertised annuity prize because of the ‘time value of money’. The actual reduction from the advertised jackpot will be declared sometime after the draw and the winner is identified as the cost of a lump sum payout is constantly changing, as a very basic guide in the past the deduction for a lump sum payout has been less than 40% of the advertised annuity prize.

Tax

US federal and state taxes are payable on large lottery winnings.
US Federal Tax: 30% on payouts greater than USD$600 for non-resident winners

US State Tax: Varies from state to state, the state we purchase our matching tickets from apply an 8% tax on payouts greater than USD$1,500

US federal and state taxes are deducted from the prize amounts prior to the payout from the state lottery office.

Promotional Prizes

We are unable to offer any local promotional prizes that are offered by the underlying lottery operators from time to time. The nine different ways for players to win a prize as listed above are the valid prizes we offer.
