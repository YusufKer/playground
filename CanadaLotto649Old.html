Canadian Lotto 6/49 Game Rules

The Canadian Lotto 6/49 is a twice weekly lottery with draws taking place every Wednesday and Saturday. For every Canadian Lotto 6/49 ticket players need to pick 6 lucky numbers from the 1-49 number pool, this includes the bonus ball number.

The Canadian Lotto 6/49 jackpot starts at $6 million and will rollover if there are no winners.

Canadian Lotto 6/49 Jackpot Rollovers

The Canadian Lotto 6/49 jackpot starts at $6 million at the beginning of every draw cycle. If the jackpot is not won there will be a rollover and the jackpot will get bigger. The rollovers will continue until there is a jackpot winner, or until the jackpot cap of $95 million is reached.

Canadian Lotto 6/49 Winnings Tax

All lottery winnings claimed through Eureka Lottos are subjected to Australian tax laws.
