US Mega Millions Game Rules

General Overview:

US Mega Millions is one of the most popular lottery games in the world, offering nine ways to win a prize.

All prizes are set cash amounts with the exception of the grand prize. The minimum USA Mega Millions jackpot is US$40 million and with no jackpot cap it continues to increase at a minimum rate of US$5 Million each draw until it is won.

Summary of Lottery Operator Rules

Selection of Numbers

To play Mega Millions, a player selects five numbers from a pool of 1 to 70 and then selects a single Mega Ball number from a pool of 1 to 25. Players may select their own numbers or use the ‘quick pick’ function to have the numbers randomly selected.

The jackpot of the underlying lottery is conducted on a pari-mutuel lottery basis, how much you win depends on the number of winning tickets in that same tier as well as the number of tickets sold in the particular underlying lottery draw.

Only one prize per game can be won in each draw, if a game qualifies as a winner across more than one prize category than the highest prize will be paid to the winning player.

Megaplier Feature

For an additional fee per play, a player can elect to add the Megaplier feature which can multiply non-jackpot prizes by 2x, 3x, 4x or 5x. The multiplier number is selected at random from a pool of balls just prior to each draw.

Draw times

Mega Millions draws are conducted twice each week, on Tuesdays and Fridays with each draw taking place at 11:00p.m. (United States Eastern Time). The draw time converts to Wednesdays and Saturdays, Australian Eastern Standard Time (AEST).

Jackpot Rollover

There is no jackpot cap on USA Mega Millions, the jackpot continues to grow until it is won. The USA Mega Millions jackpot increases by a minimum of US$5 million with each draw there is no jackpot winne

Jackpot Payout

The jackpot prize in the underlying lottery is on a pari-mutuel basis, how much you win depends on the number of winning tickets in that same tier as well as the number of tickets sold in a particular draw. Jackpot winners can elect to receive their prize as an annuity or a lump-sum payment.

Annuity

Jackpot winners who select the annuity option will receive 30 payments over 29 years, one initial payment with each following annual payment increasing at a rate of 5% per year.

The total of the 30 payments is equal to the value of the annuity.

Lump-Sum Payment

Jackpot winners who select the lump-sum payout will receive a single payment, this payment will always be an amount less than the advertised annuity prize and is equal to the cash in the Mega Millions jackpot pool.

Tax

US federal and state taxes are payable on large lottery winnings.

Promotional Prizes

We are unable to offer any local promotional prizes that are offered by the underlying lottery operators from time to time. The nine different ways for players to win a prize as listed above are the valid prizes we offer.
